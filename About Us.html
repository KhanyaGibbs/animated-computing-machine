Who We Are

DNA Brand Architects is a pioneering marketing and brand consultancy based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Founded by Sylvester Chauke, our agency is dedicated to building iconic African brands through innovation, authenticity, and strategic brilliance.

Our Mission

We are committed to crafting legacy projects that not only shift culture but also position African brands on global platforms. At the heart of DNA is the drive to empower, inspire, and challenge the norm.

Awards & Recognition

DNA Brand Architects was named one of Adweek’s Top 100 Fastest Growing Agencies. We proudly made history in 2021 as the first 100% Black-owned agency to win the Prism Award for Best Large PR Consultancy of the Year.

Our Philosophy

We believe in unexpected alignments — those rare, meaningful connections between brands and their audiences that leave lasting impact. With a team of passionate strategists, creatives, and storytellers, we push boundaries and shape narratives that matter.
